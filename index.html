About Ideathon

10-day long sub-event which will consist of 3 tracks. Participants have to put forth any AI/ML related idea that they aspire to work upon in the form of a presentation after intensive brainstorming on a predefined problem. The top 2 ideas will be given a chance to convert their idea into an open-source project with the help of experts at Omdena.
