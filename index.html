About Ideathon

The top 2 ideas will be given a chance to convert their idea into an open-source project with the help of experts at Omdena.

Event Schedule

Here is our event schedule

"The key is not to prioritize what's on your schedule, but to schedule your priorities"

21st November

Learn, Grasp and Exhibit

All teams to be provided with an inaugural session. Mandatory open-ended mentoring sessions for all teams along with an intuitive quiz for the attending teams.

22nd-25th November

Brainstorm and Produce

The teams past the first track to produce a video presentation of upto 4 minutes on their idea based on a given template on or before this date during the second track.

26th-29th November

Results announcement

15 teams to head to the third track.

30th November

Win and Implement!

The 15 teams pitch their idea to judges LIVE, of which 3 winners are chosen

Rules

The teams can consist of upto 3 students.

During the first track, only the teams which attend the mandatory mentoring session will be allowed to take up the quiz.

During the second track, a pre-recorded video presentation of the idea no longer than 4 minutes should be submitted on or before 26th November.

The presentation should strictly be based on the following template for the ideathon Template

The teams which qualify for the third track will have to pitch their idea LIVE (online/offline) in front of a group of judges.
